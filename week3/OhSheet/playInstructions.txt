Playing instructions
1. Start the application
2. Press New Game.
3. Enter Game Winning Score (must be 1000 or greater)
4. Enter the number of players (Must be 2 or greater)
5. Enter the player names
6. Player 1 begins by rolling. Game will switch turns to other player automaticaly when appropriate.
7. Roll and hold in accordance to the rules in the game rules, until the game is completed.
